Current research priorities include:

1. high-quality systematic reviews
2. pairwise and network meta-analytic approaches
3. clinically relevant topics in pain, airway management, and critical care

Collaboration is welcome for projects with clear methodology, disciplined execution, and practical clinical relevance.
